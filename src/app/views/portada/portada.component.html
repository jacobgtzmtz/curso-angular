Aplicación final del curso

Para levantar el backend hay que ejecutar el comando npm run backend

Criterios de aceptación:

  • Make 4 or more components and interconnect with each other.
  • Make 2 or more services and use in the components.
  • Make at least 4 routes(pages) with its components.
  • Make at least one pipe (filtering, sorting, or others).
  • Make at least one directive.
  • Make at least 2 forms differently (Reactive and Template ).
  • Make forms validations
  • Store the data somewhere and access them by rest API using observables or RX.
  • At least one of the components and one service needs to have unit testing at < 80% of coverage
